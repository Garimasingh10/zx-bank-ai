# ZX Bank Sub-Branches and ATM Locations

## Downtown Branch
**Address:** 123 Finance Avenue, Downtown
**Services:** Full service banking, Safety Deposit Boxes, Notary Services.
**ATM Available 24/7:** Yes
**Cash Deposit Machine:** Yes

## Northside Branch
**Address:** 456 Main Street, Northside District
**Services:** Teller services, Mortgage Consulting.
**ATM Available 24/7:** No (Accessible 8 AM - 8 PM only)
**Cash Deposit Machine:** No

## West End Kiosk (ATM Only)
**Address:** Next to West End Mall Entrance
**Services:** Cash withdrawal, balance inquiry.
**ATM Available 24/7:** Yes
**Cash Deposit Machine:** No
